Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Protecting your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to your use of the **Spheera** mobile application.

1. Data Collection

The Spheera app is designed with a **privacy-first approach**. Use our breathing tools with complete peace of mind, knowing your data is safe.

No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

No Tracking: We do not track your location, your activity within the app, or any health-related data.

No Third-Party Services: We do not use third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks that collect or transmit user data.

2. Local Storage

Any settings or data created within the app are stored exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to any servers or third parties.

3. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, Spheera is safe for users of all ages, including children.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
